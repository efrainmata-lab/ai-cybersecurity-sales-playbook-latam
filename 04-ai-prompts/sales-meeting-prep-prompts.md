# AI Prompts for Enterprise Sales Meeting Preparation

## Executive Discovery Preparation

Prompt:

"Act as a cybersecurity sales strategist preparing for an executive discovery meeting with a CISO from a large enterprise organization in Latin America. Identify likely business drivers, operational challenges, cybersecurity risks, and executive concerns. Generate strategic discovery questions and possible business outcomes."

---

## Competitive Analysis

Prompt:

"Analyze the likely strengths and weaknesses of cybersecurity competitors in an enterprise evaluation process. Focus on operational complexity, deployment speed, AI capabilities, visibility, detection quality, and executive positioning."

---

## Account Research

Prompt:

"Act as a strategic enterprise account executive. Analyze a target enterprise account in LATAM and identify likely cybersecurity priorities, digital transformation initiatives, compliance pressures, operational risks, and executive stakeholders."

---

## Executive Summary Creation

Prompt:

"Generate a concise executive summary for a cybersecurity opportunity focused on business risk reduction, operational efficiency, visibility improvement, and executive governance alignment."

---

## Objection Handling

Prompt:

"Generate executive-level responses to common cybersecurity objections related to budget constraints, deployment complexity, operational overhead, integration concerns, and ROI justification."

---

## QBR Preparation

Prompt:

"Act as a regional sales director preparing for a Quarterly Business Review with executive leadership. Summarize pipeline status, regional performance, forecast confidence, channel contribution, risks, opportunities, and strategic priorities."

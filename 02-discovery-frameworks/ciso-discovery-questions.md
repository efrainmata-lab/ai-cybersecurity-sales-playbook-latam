# CISO Discovery Questions

## Strategic Priorities

1. What are the top cybersecurity priorities for your organization this year?
2. How is cybersecurity aligned with your business transformation strategy?
3. Which risks are currently receiving the most executive attention?
4. What are the biggest operational security challenges your team faces today?

---

## Visibility and Detection

1. How confident are you in your ability to detect threats early?
2. What visibility gaps concern you the most?
3. Are you struggling with alert fatigue or false positives?
4. How do you currently prioritize incidents?

---

## Operational Challenges

1. What security processes are still highly manual?
2. Are there staffing or skill shortages impacting operations?
3. How are cloud and hybrid environments affecting security operations?
4. Which operational metrics matter most to leadership?

---

## Business Risk

1. Which business services are considered mission critical?
2. What would be the operational impact of a major breach?
3. How are you measuring cyber risk today?
4. Are compliance requirements influencing your roadmap?

---

## Executive Alignment

1. How frequently do you engage with executive leadership?
2. Which business executives influence cybersecurity decisions?
3. How does the board evaluate cybersecurity investments?
4. What business outcomes are most important for executive approval?

---

## Vendor Evaluation

1. What criteria are most important when selecting vendors?
2. What frustrations have you experienced with previous vendors?
3. Are deployment simplicity and operational efficiency priorities?
4. How important are local support and channel relationships?

---

## Closing Questions

1. What would success look like 12 months after deployment?
2. What could delay or prevent this initiative from moving forward?
3. Is there already budget allocated for this initiative?
4. Who else should be included in future conversations?

# AI-Powered Cybersecurity Sales Playbook for LATAM

A practical collection of frameworks, templates, and AI-powered prompts for enterprise cybersecurity sales leaders operating in Latin America.

This repository is designed for sales executives, country managers, channel leaders, account executives, and GTM teams working across cybersecurity, SaaS, AI-driven platforms, and enterprise technology markets.

## Purpose

The objective of this project is to provide practical resources to improve:

- Enterprise account planning
- CISO engagement
- Opportunity qualification
- Channel development
- LATAM go-to-market execution
- Executive discovery
- Pipeline governance
- AI-assisted sales workflows

## Why LATAM?

Latin America is a complex, relationship-driven, and highly diverse business region. Successful enterprise technology sales require more than product knowledge. They require local market understanding, executive trust, channel leverage, business impact alignment, and disciplined execution.

This playbook is built from real-world enterprise technology sales experience across cybersecurity, SaaS, communications, collaboration, and AI-driven platforms.

# Cybersecurity and Enterprise Sales Glossary

## Account Plan

A structured framework used to understand customer priorities, stakeholders, risks, and growth opportunities.

---

## CISO

Chief Information Security Officer.

Executive responsible for cybersecurity strategy, governance, risk management, and security operations.

---

## GTM

Go-To-Market.

The strategy used to bring products or services to market.

---

## MEDDPICC

Enterprise sales qualification methodology:

- Metrics
- Economic Buyer
- Decision Criteria
- Decision Process
- Paper Process
- Identified Pain
- Champion
- Competition

---

## MSSP

Managed Security Services Provider.

Organizations that provide outsourced cybersecurity services.

---

## Pipeline Governance

The disciplined management of sales opportunities, forecasting, risk evaluation, and execution tracking.

---

## SaaS

Software as a Service.

Cloud-delivered software subscription model.

---

## SOC

Security Operations Center.

Team responsible for monitoring, detecting, and responding to cybersecurity threats.

Security model based on continuous verification and least-privilege access.

# Partner Recruitment Framework for LATAM

## Objective

Build a scalable, trusted, and execution-focused channel ecosystem capable of generating pipeline, accelerating market penetration, and expanding regional influence.

---

## Ideal Partner Profile

### Business Characteristics

- Enterprise customer base
- Security practice maturity
- Technical certification capabilities
- Existing executive relationships
- Regional coverage
- Financial stability

### Strategic Alignment

- Cybersecurity focus
- SaaS experience
- Cloud capabilities
- Services delivery capacity
- Executive engagement model

---

## Partner Segmentation

| Tier | Profile | Expected Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| Strategic | Regional influence | High pipeline and executive engagement |
| Growth | Emerging regional partners | Territory expansion |
| Specialized | Vertical expertise | Technical specialization |
